The Tyranny of Virtue

Welcome back to Britannia, Avatar. The land you once saved has fallen under a dark shadow. Lord British is lost in the Underworld, and his regent, Lord Blackthorn, enforces the Virtues with an iron fist, twisted by the influence of the three malevolent Shadowlords.

This interactive guide is your companion in the struggle to liberate Britannia. It's designed to provide quick, accessible information when you need it most. Use the navigation to explore potential party members, learn survival essentials, track your progress through the main quests, and reference critical information like spells and mantras. Your goal is not just to rescue a king, but to restore the very soul of Britannia—the principle that true virtue must be a choice, not a law.

The Resistance Survival Kit

Success in *Ultima V* is about more than just combat prowess; it's about preparation and resourcefulness. This section provides a quick reference for the core principles of survival in Blackthorn's Britannia, strategies for funding your resistance, and a checklist of the most essential gear you'll need to find to complete your quest.

Essential Gear

Magic Carpet

Found in Lord British's chambers. Allows flight over shallow water, swamps, and hills. Essential for traversing Stonegate.

Skull Keys

An infinite supply can be found daily in a tree in Minoc's northwest corner. Opens any magically locked door.

Grappling Hook

Given by Lord Michael in Empath Abbey. Necessary for climbing mountains and accessing key areas.

Magic Axes

The best ranged weapon. Found in Lord British's treasury or purchased in Yew. They return after being thrown.

Funding & Survival

Core Tenets

  • Save constantly: Avoid losing hours of progress.
  • Avoid death: Resurrection causes permanent XP loss.
  • Manage resources: Food is consumed with every step.

Economic Strategy

The most lucrative way to fund the resistance is by repeatedly looting Lord British's castle treasury. Use Skull Keys to enter the basement, take everything, exit the castle, and re-enter. The treasury will be restocked. Be aware this will lower your Karma.

Navigation

Horses provide faster overland travel. A frigate, won from pirates, is needed for sea voyages. Moongates offer magical transport at night, with destinations determined by the two moons.

The Path to Liberation

Your journey to free Britannia is long and perilous. It involves dismantling Blackthorn's regime, rescuing the true king, and restoring the meaning of Virtue. This quest log is divided into the major acts of your adventure. Begin by restoring the Shrines, then shatter the Shadowlords' influence, recover the king's regalia, and finally, prepare for the descent into Doom.

The Path of Virtue

Restoring the Shrines of the Eight Virtues is the primary way to increase your party's attributes and is required to learn the final Word of Power. Each shrine follows a four-step process.

1. Learn the Mantra: Converse with townsfolk in the virtue's associated city.
2. Meditate at the Shrine: Use the mantra to meditate for three cycles to begin the quest.
3. Journey to the Codex: Travel to the Isle of the Avatar to receive wisdom.
4. Attain Enlightenment: Return to the shrine and meditate again to complete the quest and gain an attribute increase.

Completing all eight quests will grant you the Word of Power for Dungeon Doom: VERAMOCOR.

Act I: Shatter the Shadows

The three Shadowlords must be destroyed before Lord British can be rescued. They cannot be defeated in normal combat. Their destruction requires a precise ritual.

Step 1: Learn Their True Names

  • Faulinei (Falsehood): Speak to the twin sisters in Cove.
  • Astaroth (Hatred): Learn from the gargoyle Sin'Vraal in the eastern desert.
  • Nosfentor (Cowardice): Revealed by various knowledgeable NPCs.

Step 2: Retrieve Their Shards from the Underworld

Enter the dungeons using the correct Words of Power to find the shards from which the Shadowlords were born.

  • Shard of Falsehood: Beneath Dungeon Deceit (Word: FALLAX). Requires Grappling Hook and Magic Carpet.
  • Shard of Hatred: Beneath Dungeons Wrong/Covetous (Words: MALUM/AVIDUS). Requires Grappling Hook.
  • Shard of Cowardice: Beneath Dungeon Hythloth (Word: IGNAVUS). Requires Magic Carpet and the 'In Por' (Blink) spell.

Step 3: Perform the Ritual of Banishment

At the keep corresponding to the principle they oppose, summon the Shadowlord by shouting its name near the sacred flame. When it steps onto the flame, use its shard on the flame to destroy it forever.

  • Faulinei (Falsehood) → Destroyed at the Lyceum (Truth).
  • Astaroth (Hatred) → Destroyed at Empath Abbey (Love).
  • Nosfentor (Cowardice) → Destroyed at Serpent's Hold (Courage).

Act II: Recover the Regalia

With the Shadowlords gone, you must find Lord British's four royal artifacts. These are essential for surviving the final dungeon.

The Sceptre

Location: Stonegate.
Function: Dispels magical fields.
Requirement: Magic Carpet to bypass deadly floor traps.

The Crown

Location: Rooftop of Blackthorn's Palace.
Function: Negates enemy magic.
Requirement: Skull Keys and the Black Badge to move freely.

The Amulet

Location: Deep in the Underworld, where the king's knights fell (accessible via Dungeon Destard or Spiritwood waterfall).
Function: Reveals the hidden entrance to Dungeon Doom.

The Sandalwood Box

Location: Lord British's private study.
Function: Proves your identity to the king.
Requirement: Play the tune "Stones" (6-7-8-9-8-7-8-7-6-7-6-5-3) on the castle harpsichord.

Act III: The Final Descent into Doom

The final challenge. This is a one-way trip, so make a permanent save before you enter. Ensure you have all four royal artifacts and the Sandalwood Box, and that the Shadowlords are defeated.

Final Preparations

  • All 3 Shadowlords must be destroyed.
  • Possess the Sceptre, Crown, Amulet, and Sandalwood Box.
  • The Avatar must be at least Level 8.
  • Stockpile healing potions and resurrection spells.

The Rescue

The entrance to Doom is in the central Underworld, hidden in darkness (use the Amulet). Speak the Word of Power VERAMOCOR to enter.

  1. Use the Sceptre to dispel the ethereal walls at the entrance.
  2. Navigate the labyrinthine levels, using the Crown to survive rooms filled with powerful magic-using monsters.
  3. On the final level, find the hidden pit leading to Lord British's prison.
  4. Lord British is trapped in a mirror. Step through and give him the Sandalwood Box to free him.
  5. Use the Orb of the Moons he gives you to escape the collapsing dungeon.
